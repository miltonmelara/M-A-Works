General description

In this area our mission is to create the best courses on each of the following topics, and more.

Cooking

This type course will teach about cooking techniques and recipes. We can divide this team into different areas based on the type of food that the course will teach about.

Programming languages

This type of course will teach about coding languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, etc. We can also divide the course into different areas based on the language, usage, and level.

Drawing

This course will cover drawing techniques and types of drawing.

Design

This course will cover the principles of positioning, colors, etc.

Music

This course will teach principles of learning to play certain instruments.

Lettering

This course will cover the basic techniques of writing letters.

General requirements

  • General knowledge of the topic of the area
  • Experience in the subject
  • Currently be gaining more knowledge in the subject
  • Be at least 15 years old
